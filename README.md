# SpillAura

Philips Hue ambient lighting for macOS.

## Features

- **Screen Sync** — captures your display in real-time and pushes matching colors to your lights at up to 60fps
- **Aura mode** — animated color palettes that cycle through your lights independently of screen content
- Automatic bridge discovery via mDNS
- Resumes streaming after sleep/wake
- Lives in your menu bar

## Requirements

- macOS 14 or later
- Philips Hue Bridge (v2)
- Philips Hue Play lights configured in an Entertainment Group

## Setup

1. Open SpillAura — it will discover your bridge automatically
2. Press the link button on your bridge when prompted to pair
3. Grant Screen Recording permission when asked (System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording)
4. Select a mode and press play

## Notes

- Entertainment Groups must be created in the official Hue app before first use
- Screen Recording permission is required only for Screen Sync mode
